Welcome to our not so complete guide of local hang outs for the self-proclaimed cool kid in Oxford. We have craeted a list of restaurants, cafes, bars and miscellaneous others for when you need to escape from reality or need to be reminded just how cool you are or simply want to dazzle your friends with your knowledge of the chiccest places to be.

The movement

Our aim is to mobolise an army of super cool kids to support local businesses and break obstinate division of "town and gown".

Places

Restaurants

Edamame, A family run restaurant serving authentic japanese: super cosy and pretty cheap. The thursday sushi nights are not to be missed.

Turl street kitchen, A bit pricey but the menu is original enough that they can get away with it.

SoJo, Litterally, the best dimsum in Oxford!

Buongiorno e Buonasera, Authentic Italian in every sense; equiped with an Italian minimart.

Unclassified

Pheonix picturehouse, Indie and foreign cinema house showing mainstream films and lesser publicised foreign films and documentaries.

The Oxford Foundry, Co-working space, welcoming students from acorss the University who interested in exploring the entrepreneurial ecosystem.
