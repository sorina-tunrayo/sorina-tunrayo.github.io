Welcome to our not so complete guide of local hangouts for the self-proclaimed cool kid in Oxford. We have created a list of restaurants, cafes, bars and miscellaneous others for when you need to escape from reality or simply want to dazzle your friends with your knowledge of the chiccest places to be.

The movement

Our aim is to mobilise an army of super cool kids to support local businesses and break the obstinate division of "town and gown".

Tunrayo: As a new member of the Oxford graduate community, I, like so many others have also been overwhelmed by the huge number of activities that the colleges and the Universities provide. Futhermore, my department is exactly 5 minutes from my bed and my college, well...It is so easy to be trapped in the very circle that inevitably forms and thus never see anything beyond its radius.

Trina: I love food so much I spent a term as the Cherwell's Food and Drink editor, but I am far too lazy to cook. So after almost four years here I've found one or two great places I'd like to share with everyone.

Fran: I also love food and, having been here nearly 3 years, I know some info about places to eat in Oxford.

We say, who said you can't have "town and gown"?
